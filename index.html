How to Download and Install MetaMask Extension on Chrome

NM

This guide shows you, step by step, how to safely download, install, and set up the MetaMask browser extension on Google Chrome. You‚Äôll also learn how to create or import a wallet and how to protect your Secret Recovery Phrase.

1. What Is MetaMask and Why Use It on Chrome?

MetaMask is a non-custodial crypto wallet and gateway to Web3. As a Chrome extension, it lets you:

  • Store and send cryptocurrencies (such as ETH and many other tokens).
  • Connect to decentralized applications (dApps) directly from your browser.
  • Sign transactions securely without sharing your private keys with websites.

Because it runs as a browser extension, MetaMask can integrate directly with websites and Web3 apps while keeping your keys encrypted on your device.

2. Safety Checklist Before You Install MetaMask

Before installing the MetaMask Chrome extension, go through this quick safety checklist:

  • Use the official website only: type https://metamask.io manually into your browser‚Äôs address bar. Avoid clicking random ads or unknown links.
  • Check the Chrome Web Store listing: the publisher should be MetaMask, and the extension should have a large number of users and reviews.
  • Never share your Secret Recovery Phrase (seed phrase): MetaMask support and legitimate websites will never ask for it.
  • Keep Chrome updated: newer versions of Chrome are more secure and compatible with extensions.

If anything looks suspicious, stop and re-check the URL and extension details before continuing.

3. Step 1 ‚Äì Open the Official MetaMask Download Page

  1. Open Google Chrome on your computer.
  2. In the address bar, type https://metamask.io and press Enter.
  3. On the MetaMask homepage, click the button in the header that says ‚ÄúDownload‚Äù or ‚ÄúGet MetaMask‚Äù.
  4. On the download page, choose Chrome as your platform. This will redirect you to MetaMask‚Äôs listing on the official Chrome Web Store.

If you are not redirected, you can also open the Chrome Web Store and search for ‚ÄúMetaMask‚Äù, then select the official extension published by MetaMask.

4. Step 2 ‚Äì Add MetaMask Extension to Chrome

Once you are on the MetaMask page in the Chrome Web Store:

  1. Click the ‚ÄúAdd to Chrome‚Äù button on the right side of the page.
  2. Chrome will show a confirmation popup. Click ‚ÄúAdd extension‚Äù.
  3. Chrome will download the extension and install it automatically. This usually takes a few seconds.

After installation, a new MetaMask tab will typically open automatically to start the setup process. If it doesn‚Äôt, you can open it from the extensions menu (see the next section).

5. Step 3 ‚Äì Pin MetaMask to the Chrome Toolbar

Pinning MetaMask keeps it visible and easy to open in one click. To pin the extension:

  1. At the top-right of Chrome, click the puzzle piece icon (Extensions).
  2. In the dropdown list, find MetaMask.
  3. Click the pin icon next to MetaMask. The fox logo should now appear on your main toolbar.
  4. Click the MetaMask fox icon to open the extension window.

This makes it much easier to access MetaMask whenever you need it.

6. Step 4 ‚Äì Create or Import Your MetaMask Wallet

When MetaMask opens for the first time (either in a new tab or in the extension popup), you will see a welcome screen.

  1. Click ‚ÄúGet Started‚Äù.
  2. Choose one of the two options:
    • Create a new wallet ‚Äì if you are completely new to MetaMask.
    • Import wallet ‚Äì if you already have a MetaMask or compatible wallet and you have your Secret Recovery Phrase.

6.1 Create a New MetaMask Wallet

If you choose to create a new wallet, follow these steps:

  1. MetaMask may ask if you want to share anonymous usage data to help improve the product. Choose ‚ÄúI Agree‚Äù or ‚ÄúNo Thanks‚Äù. Your wallet works either way.
  2. Create a strong password for unlocking MetaMask on this device. Use a combination of:
    • Uppercase and lowercase letters
    • Numbers
    • Special characters (e.g., !@#$%)
  3. Confirm the password and agree to the terms of use, then click ‚ÄúCreate‚Äù or ‚ÄúCreate a new wallet‚Äù.
  4. MetaMask will show a short video or explanation about the Secret Recovery Phrase. Watch/read this carefully.
  5. Click to reveal your Secret Recovery Phrase (12 or 24 words, depending on your wallet).

6.2 Import an Existing Wallet

If you already have a wallet and want to import it into MetaMask on Chrome:

  1. Select ‚ÄúImport wallet‚Äù on the first screen.
  2. Enter your Secret Recovery Phrase in the correct word order. Make sure no one can see your screen.
  3. Set a new password for MetaMask on this device (this password is local to the browser and does not replace your recovery phrase).
  4. Confirm and complete the import process.

Once imported, your existing accounts and balances will appear inside MetaMask, depending on the network you select (e.g., Ethereum, Linea, etc.).

7. Step 5 ‚Äì Secure Your Secret Recovery Phrase

Your Secret Recovery Phrase (seed phrase) is the master key to your wallet. Anyone who has it can access and move your funds.

Best practices for securing your phrase:

  • Write it down on paper and store it in a safe place (or multiple places).
  • Do not save it in plain text on your computer, cloud storage, or email.
  • Never share it with anyone, even if they claim to be support staff.
  • Consider using a fireproof/waterproof container or a metal backup for long-term storage.

During setup, MetaMask will ask you to confirm your Secret Recovery Phrase by selecting the words in the correct order. This ensures you have saved it correctly.

8. Step 6 ‚Äì Check Extension Status and Basic Troubleshooting

If you have followed the steps but still cannot see or use MetaMask, try the checks below.

8.1 Make Sure MetaMask Is Enabled in Chrome

  1. In Chrome, type chrome://extensions/ into the address bar and press Enter.
  2. Find MetaMask in the list of installed extensions.
  3. Ensure the toggle switch is turned On. If it is off, click it to enable the extension.
  4. If necessary, click Details to review permissions.

8.2 MetaMask Tab or Popup Doesn‚Äôt Open

  • Close and reopen Chrome, then click the MetaMask icon again.
  • Make sure no aggressive popup blocker or security extension is blocking MetaMask windows.
  • If the problem persists, try disabling other extensions temporarily to identify conflicts.

8.3 Manual Installation (Advanced Only)

MetaMask can also be installed manually using a downloaded ZIP file and the ‚ÄúLoad unpacked‚Äù feature in chrome://extensions, but this is intended only for advanced users who understand the security risks and know how to verify official builds.

For most users, the safest option is to install MetaMask only via https://metamask.io and the official Chrome Web Store listing.

9. Basic Next Steps After Installing MetaMask

Once MetaMask is installed and your wallet is set up, you can:

  • View your wallet address and receive crypto from exchanges or other wallets.
  • Send crypto to other addresses (double-check addresses before sending!).
  • Connect to dApps such as DeFi platforms, NFT marketplaces, and Web3 games.
  • Switch networks (e.g., Ethereum Mainnet, testnets, or other supported chains).

Always start with small test transactions and never invest more than you can afford to lose when exploring new dApps or networks.

10. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

10.1 Is MetaMask free to use on Chrome?

Yes. Installing and using the MetaMask extension is free. You only pay network fees (gas fees) when you send transactions on blockchain networks such as Ethereum.

10.2 Can I use MetaMask on other browsers?

Yes. MetaMask also supports other major browsers like Firefox, Brave, Edge, and Opera. The installation steps are similar, but this guide focuses specifically on Google Chrome.

10.3 How do I update MetaMask on Chrome?

MetaMask usually updates automatically through the Chrome Web Store. To check manually, go to chrome://extensions/, enable Developer mode, and click ‚ÄúUpdate‚Äù. Chrome will then update all extensions, including MetaMask, to the latest version if available.

10.4 What if MetaMask suddenly disappears from my toolbar?

First, click the puzzle icon and see if MetaMask is still installed and just not pinned. If it‚Äôs not there, open chrome://extensions/ and check whether it has been disabled or removed. If needed, reinstall from the official MetaMask download page.

10.5 Can MetaMask recover my wallet if I lose my Secret Recovery Phrase?

No. MetaMask cannot recover your wallet for you. The Secret Recovery Phrase is the only way to restore access to your funds. This is why storing it securely is absolutely critical.

11. Summary

To install MetaMask on Chrome, you visit the official MetaMask website, go to the download page, add the extension from the Chrome Web Store, pin it to your toolbar, and then create or import a wallet. The most important part of the process is securing your Secret Recovery Phrase and verifying that you are always using the official MetaMask extension and website.
